Traveling to Tokyo

Visit some of Tokyo's most popular and sacred temples, like the Sensō-ji Temple.
Explore and eat delicious food and desserts at numerous, amazing restaurants and street vendors,like the Tsukiji Market
Travel throughout Tokyo using one of the city's most popular transportation systems: the Train system. Tokyo also has some cartoon themed trains, like the Gudetama train above!
Tokyo is home to many unique hotels, like capsule hotels.
